About

Clay & Coffee is a unique pottery studio and cafe that brings together the joy of creating art with the comfort of great coffee. Founded in 2020, we've become a beloved community space where creativity flows as freely as our freshly brewed coffee.

Our studio offers guided pottery workshops for all skill levels, from beginners trying their hand at the wheel for the first time to experienced artists looking to refine their craft. After a creative session, unwind at our cafe with artisanal coffee and homemade treats.

We're Building Hyderabad's Creative Community

GoOut Hyd was born from a simple idea: to connect people with experiences that matter. We believe that the best moments happen when you step out, explore, and engage with your city's vibrant creative culture.

Our Story

It all started in 2023 when Wilson, a Hyderabad native with a passion for live events, noticed a gap. The city had incredible independent cafes and talented artists, but there was no easy way for people to discover them.

Wilson began organizing small live music nights at local cafes. As the events grew in popularity, cafe owners started reaching out, asking him to host events at their venues too. That's when the idea for GoOut Hyd took shape.

"I wanted to create a platform where cafe owners could thrive, artists could find their audience, and people could discover experiences that make life more colorful."

— Wilson, Founder
